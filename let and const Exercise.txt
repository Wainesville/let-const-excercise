
## **Quiz**

- What is the difference between ***var*** and ***let***?
====>you can't redeclare let and it creates a block scope
- What is the difference between **var** and ***const***?
====> you can't redeclare const and it creates a block scope
- What is the difference between **let** and **const**?
=====> you can reassign let but cant with const
- What is hoisting?
==========> when the variable is lifted to the top of scope and can be access the name
and  it's value before it is used later on.
